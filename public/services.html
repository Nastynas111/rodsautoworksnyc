Our Services

Complete auto body and repair solutions — from minor dents to full collision rebuilds.

🔨
01

Collision Repair

Whether it's a minor fender bender or a major accident, our collision repair team has the experience and equipment to restore your vehicle to pre-accident condition. We use computerized frame measuring systems and factory-spec repair techniques.

  • Frame straightening & alignment
  • Structural panel replacement
  • Unibody & full-frame repairs
  • Post-repair alignment check
  • OEM-grade parts sourcing
🎨
02

Auto Body & Paint

Our paint department uses advanced computerized color-matching technology to ensure a flawless finish every time. We apply premium automotive paints in a climate-controlled environment, delivering results that match or exceed the original factory finish.

  • Computerized paint color matching
  • Full respray & panel painting
  • Rust treatment & prevention
  • Clear coat & protective finish
  • Luxury & exotic vehicle specialists
🪟
03

Window Tinting

Professional window tinting with premium film brands. Our installers are meticulous — no bubbles, no peeling, clean edges every time. We offer a range of tint levels to suit your style and comply with NY state regulations.

  • UV & heat-rejection film
  • Multiple tint percentages available
  • Ceramic & standard film options
  • NY legal compliance guidance
  • Rear, side & windshield tinting
📋
04

Insurance Claims

Dealing with insurance after an accident is stressful. We handle the process from start to finish — documentation, estimates, direct billing, and communication with adjusters — so you can focus on getting back on the road.

  • Direct insurance billing
  • All major carriers accepted
  • Detailed damage documentation
  • Adjuster coordination
  • Supplement claims assistance
💧
05

Dent Removal

Paintless dent repair (PDR) is the most efficient way to remove minor dents and dings without disrupting your original paint. We use specialized tools and techniques to massage panels back into their original shape.

  • Paintless dent repair (PDR)
  • Hail damage restoration
  • Door ding removal
  • Crease & dent reshaping
  • Same-day service for small repairs
🔍
06

Glass Repair

A cracked windshield is a safety hazard. We provide fast, reliable windshield chip repair and full glass replacement. Our installations meet or exceed OEM standards with a proper seal to keep the elements out.

  • Windshield chip & crack repair
  • Full windshield replacement
  • Side & rear glass replacement
  • ADAS camera recalibration
  • Watertight seal guarantee
✨
07

Detailing

Make your car look and smell like new again. Our detailing packages cover interior and exterior — hand wash, clay bar, polishing, wax, leather conditioning, deep interior cleaning, and more.

  • Full interior & exterior detail
  • Clay bar & paint decontamination
  • Machine polish & wax
  • Leather cleaning & conditioning
  • Engine bay cleaning available
🛡️

We Work With Your Insurance

Don't stress about the paperwork. Rods AutoWorks works directly with all major insurance carriers — Geico, State Farm, Allstate, Progressive, Liberty Mutual, and more. We handle the claims process so you can focus on what matters.
