About the Shop

Astoria's Trusted Auto Body Shop

Located on the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway in Astoria, Rods AutoWorks has built a reputation for honest work, fair prices, and results that speak for themselves. From luxury sedans to work trucks, we treat every vehicle like it's our own.
